About Me

I am a creative analytical thinker, strong writer, and servant leader. I graduated from the University of Georgia with a Bachelor of Arts in History and a Certificate in Museum Studies. Seeking to understand how people experience history in a digital environment, I completed the User Experience and Interface Design Bootcamp at Georgia Tech. Among many projects, I collaborated alongside a team virtually to design a mobile app for a local animal shelter. I also have experience in communications, writing press releases and blog posts, as an intern in the Public Relations Department at the Georgia Museum of Art. I work best in a fast-paced and productive work environment.
